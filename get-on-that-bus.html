Get On That Bus App

Description

An app that displays all the bus stops in Chicago area. It displays the bus stations with pins and also show the list of bus stops.
The app also implements a way to tap on the pin accessory view or tableview cell and take you to the detail view controller that would show more information on that particular bus stop.
Chicago also has two unique types of bus stops, one called Metra and another one called Pace.The map shows those pins in different colors.

Technology

  • Objetive C
  • CoreLocation
  • iOS
  • MapKit
  • CLGeocoder
  • CLPlacemark
